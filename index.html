Privacy Policy.

Effective Date: April 8, 2026

At Winpath Diagnostics, we recognize that health information is the most sensitive form of personal data. As an ISO 15189:2022 certified laboratory, we are committed to the highest standards of data integrity and patient confidentiality.

1.1 Data We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide accurate diagnostic services:

  • Personal Identity: Name, age, gender, and date of birth.
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address, and residential address (for home collection).
  • Clinical Information: Physician referrals, clinical history, and biological samples.
  • Financial Data: Payment details for billing purposes (processed through encrypted gateways).

1.2 Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed for:

  • Performing requested diagnostic tests and generating digital reports.
  • Scheduling and executing professional home collection services.
  • Internal quality control and adherence to ISO 15189:2022 standards.
  • Legal Mandates: Reporting "Notifiable Diseases" (e.g., Tuberculosis, Malaria, COVID-19) to relevant Indian government health authorities as required by law.

1.3 Data Sharing and Disclosure

Winpath Diagnostics does not sell your data. Disclosure occurs only:

  • To the referring clinician or hospital mentioned during registration.
  • To accredited outsourced laboratories if a specialized test is not performed in-house.
  • When required by a court of law or a government regulatory agency.

1.4 Data Security

We implement "Reasonable Security Practices" as defined under the IT Rules, 2011. This includes end-to-end encryption for digital reports and restricted access to the Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS).

1.5 Your Rights (Under DPDPA 2023)

Patients have the right to request a summary of personal data processed, correct clinical records, or withdraw consent for data processing.

1.6 Grievance Redressal

Any grievances regarding data processing can be directed to:
Email: grievance@winpath.in
Address: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
